Welcome to the University of Ghana Computer Science Department! We are thrilled to have you explore the exciting world of computer science with us. Our department is committed to fostering excellence in education and cutting-edge research.

Overview of the Computer Science Department:

Established in 1947, the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana has been at the forefront of innovation and education in the field. With a rich history and a commitment to excellence, we take pride in our contributions to the rapidly evolving world of technology.

Student Resources:

Your success is our priority. Take advantage of academic advising services, tutoring programs, and a range of resources to support your learning journey. Explore internship opportunities and connect with our career services for guidance on launching your career in computer science.
